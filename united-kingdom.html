United Kingdom

The UK has some world famous tourist attractions and scenery is stunning and varied. The cities are vibrant and bustling, while the countryside is totally breathtaking, from rolling fields and hills, snowcapped mountains, forests, beaches and lakes, there really is something for everyone! Nightlife in the UK is a huge part of the student scene, while there are plenty of sports and other activities to occupy you.

Why Choose UK

The UK is home to seven out of the top 10 universities in Europe. And the universities of Oxford, Cambridge and London (Imperial College) are ranked amongst the top 10 in the World! Although the UK has only 1% of the world’s population, it undertakes 5% of the world’s scientific research and produces 14% of the world’s most highly cited academic papers. By studying in the UK, you have the opportunity to graduate from one of the best education systems in the world!

Shorter Course Durations

Undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the UK tend to be shorter than in other countries. You can earn a Bachelors degree in 3 years and Masters Degree in 1 year. Thus enabling you to join the workforce faster or giving you one full year to explore and enjoy life before you take the plunge. The money saved on tuition fees for the additional years can be utilized for better purposes like starting your own business!

Cultural Diversity

UK is a melting pot of several cultures. The cultural diversity of life in British higher education is unrivalled. From cosmopolitan cities like London, Cardiff, Belfast and Glasgow, to historic counties like Warwickshire and Yorkshire, the UK is a place of contrasts and culture, where ancient buildings sit alongside contemporary architecture.

Excellent Teaching Format

UK institutes are globally known for high quality.UK universities place greater focus on smaller group tutorials and one-to-one tuition to ensure students are able to grasp the subject thoroughly.

Lower costs for high values

Tuition costs in the UK are significantly lower than in most other developed countries.

Discover, Create, Innovate!

The UK is one of the most exciting and productive places in the world for research, creativity and innovation. If you have a passion for learning and want to be at the forefront of your subject, this is the place to be.

Students studying PG level programmes at Universities  can work up to 20 hours a week during term time and full time (ie 40 hours) during vacations.

Dependents : Student who enrol for the courses for Post Graduate Certificate, Post graduation Certificate in Education, Post Graduate Diploma of Masters degree at a university for which the duration should be more than 12 months can sponsor a dependent. The dependents will be allowed to work.

Those graduating from a UK university with a recognized degree can apply for  Tier 2 – Sponsored Skilled Category Visa , before their student visa expires. The normal Tier 2 requirements will apply and the Advantage of this scheme is that the Employer will not have to undergo The Resident Labour Market Test.

Genuine student entrepreneurs with a great idea can also apply for Tier 1 (Graduate Entrepreneur Category visa) which allows them to  stay on in the UK to develop their business proposition after completion of their course.

International students graduating from a program of 2 years or more in length would be eligible for a 3 year work permit after graduation with no restrictions on the types of employment and no requirement for a job offer. The experience gained would provide them an opportunity to work in an international setting and at the same time allow them to earn the money invested.

Canada is encouraging International Students to stay back after their studies. The new Work Permit Program states that International students who have studied for 2 years can obtain an Open Work Permit with no restrictions on the type of employment and no requirements of a Job offer upto 3 years.
