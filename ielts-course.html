IELTS Course Overview

IELTS preparation involves understanding the test format, how it is marked and then making a plan to achieve your desired score. In order to do this, you will need to improve your English, practice test skills and join a preparation course to check your progress before you register for the test. Find out how to improve your preparation and accelerate your journey.

Course Features

For the last few years, We have helped students, business persons, tourists, clients with medical needs.

Certified Trainers

Our PTE Faculties Are Certified Trainers, who have undergone training with Pearson, the creators of PTE Academic Test.

Flexible Batches

We run multiple batches in a day. Clients can select a time of their choice. Also, special weekend batches are held for professionals.

Best in-class Facilities

Every student will be provided a PC with headphones in our state-of-the-art PTE Lab. Sessions are held using Hi-tech equipment.

In-house Materials

We have developed our own, one-of-its-kind, comprehensive material, to prepare test takers for the actual PTE Academic Test.

In-house Practice Tests

We have made 10 high quality Practice Tests, keeping in mind the quality and feel of the actual test.

Master Class

A first-of-its-kind, special pre-exam Master Class is held by Master Trainers, one-on-one with every student, a day before the actual test.

Techniques & Strategies

Enough strategies will be shared with you throughout the coaching duration, right from the 1stday, so that you can tackle the test confidently.

Guaranteed Results

Finally, Results speak for themselves. If you follow our tips and strategies, you will definitely get your desired result!

Preparation Tips & Tricks

The four courses from the British Council give you all the information and practice you need to approach all four parts of IELTS with confidence that you can achieve the band score you need

  • Understanding and describing charts and data
  • Writing about trends
  • Planning an essay
  • The grammar and vocabulary you need
  • Reading about unfamiliar topics in English
  • Improving your pronunciation
